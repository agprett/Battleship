Battleship
Instructions:
Hit "Player Ready?" button to cover the boards
CAUTION: Boards are locked in after that button is hit
Click squares to place ships
Enter coordinate and hit the "ENTER" key to input!
Hit the "Shoot" button to fire!
Red is a hit, green is a miss
Shots are logged in the square on the right
